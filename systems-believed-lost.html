Theorem Provers believed lost

The following theorem provers are not featured in the Theorem Prover Museum but are believed lost - in the sense that we have looked for them and could not find the sources.

We list the systems and detail what we have done to find them. Should you have other leads, please contact Michael Kohlhase or just open an issue on our tracker.
